Bread Recipe

Banana Bread Recipe From Simply Recipies https://www.simplyrecipes.com/recipes/banana_bread/

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cups of Flour
  • 1 Teaspoon of Baking Soda
  • One pinch of Salt
  • 3/4 Cup Sugar
  • 1/3 Cup Melted Butter
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1 1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla
  • 3 Very Ripe Bannanas

Directions

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.

In a large mixing bowl Mix the bannas and wet ingredients thoroughly.

Add the sugar to the wet mix.

Mix the dry ingredients in a seperate bowl.

Add the dry mixture to the wet mixture mixing till the two are well mixed

Pour the batter into a lightly greased baking pan.

Bake at 350F for 50-60 min or untill the smoke detector goes off.

Allow to cool down, covered, overnight.
